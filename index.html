Melon Day

Melon Day is a national holiday of Turkmenistan celebrating the glorious Turkmen musk melon.

It falls on the second Sunday of August and is the cause for much celebration.

MelonDayNYC is a non-profit organization dedicated to sharing the joy (and fruit) of this tasty holiday in New York.

Join us on August 8th, 2021 for our 5th annual celebration of Melons in Prospect Park.

COVID-19

We hope that COVID-19 not be a problem for this year's celebration. As an outdoor gathering among vaccinated people we expect that this event can be held safely according to local and health national guidelines.
